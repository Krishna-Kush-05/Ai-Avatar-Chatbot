Q: What is MIT Academy of Engineering?
A: MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE) is an autonomous engineering college established in 1999. It is part of the MAEER's MIT Group of Institutions, Pune. The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).

Q: What is the vision and mission of MITAOE?
A: The vision of MITAOE is to be a recognized center of excellence in education and research, producing globally competent professionals. The mission is to provide a student-centric learning environment, foster research and innovation, and build strong industry-academia collaborations.

Q: Is MITAOE an autonomous college?
A: Yes, MITAOE is an autonomous institute, which allows it to have a flexible and industry-relevant curriculum.

Q: What are the accreditations of MITAOE?
A: MITAOE is accredited with an **'A' Grade by NAAC** (National Assessment and Accreditation Council). Several of its programs, including Chemical, Civil, and Computer Engineering, are accredited by the **NBA** (National Board of Accreditation).

Q: What is the NIRF ranking of MITAOE?
A: In the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) Innovation rankings for 2020, MITAOE was placed in the **26-50** band.

Q: What undergraduate engineering programs (B.Tech) does MITAOE offer?
A: MITAOE offers a range of B.Tech specializations, including:
- Computer Engineering
- Computer Engineering (Software Engineering)
- Information Technology
- CSE (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning)
- CSE (Data Science)
- Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Chemical Engineering

Q: What is the eligibility criteria for B.Tech admission?
A: The candidate must have passed the 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. The minimum required marks are **45%** for the General category and **40%** for reserved categories of Maharashtra State.

Q: Which entrance exams are accepted for B.Tech admissions?
A: Admissions are based on the scores of the **MHT-CET** (for Maharashtra state candidates) or **JEE Main** (for All India candidates).

Q: What is the admission process for B.Tech?
A: The admission process is conducted through the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Government of Maharashtra. Candidates must register on the official DTE website and select MITAOE as a preference during the counseling rounds.

Q: Are there lateral entry (Direct Second Year) admissions available?
A: Yes, MITAOE offers Direct Second Year B.Tech admissions for candidates who have completed a Diploma in Engineering/Technology with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).

Q: What postgraduate (M.Tech) programs are available?
A: MITAOE offers M.Tech specializations in:
- Computer Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering

Q: What is the eligibility for M.Tech programs?
A: Candidates must have a Bachelor's Degree in the relevant field of Engineering/Technology with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). A valid **GATE** score is required for admission.

Q: What is the approximate fee structure for the B.Tech program?
A: For the B.Tech program, the total tuition fees for the entire 4-year duration range from approximately **₹7.6 Lakhs to ₹7.7 Lakhs** for the General category. For the first year, the fee is around ₹1.92 Lakhs. Fees are subject to change as per the guidelines of the Fee Regulating Authority, Maharashtra State.

Q: What is included in the fee structure?
A: The annual fee typically includes a Tuition Fee and a Development Fee. Additional charges like university fees may apply.

Q: Are there any fee concessions available?
A: Yes, fee concessions are available for reserved category students (SC/ST/SBC) and for students admitted through the Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFWS) as per government norms. For these categories, the tuition fee is significantly reduced.

Q: Does the college offer scholarships?
A: The college facilitates various government scholarships and schemes. Students can apply for scholarships based on their category, merit, and financial background through the national and state scholarship portals.

Q: How are the placements at MITAOE?
A: MITAOE has a strong and dedicated placement cell with a good track record. A majority of eligible students, often cited between 70-90%, get placed through on-campus drives.

Q: What is the highest and average package offered?
A: For the 2025 batch (placements ongoing), the **highest package** recorded is **₹23.2 LPA**. The **average package** is approximately **₹7.17 LPA**. These figures can vary year to year.

Q: Which companies recruit from MITAOE?
A: A wide range of companies visit the campus for recruitment. Top recruiters include **PhonePe, Cisco, Infosys, TCS, ZS Associates, IBM, Cognizant, and Accenture**.

Q: Do students get internship opportunities?
A: Yes, the college encourages students to take up internships to gain practical industry experience. The placement cell also assists students in securing internships. Many students have received internships in countries like Germany and Japan.

Q: Does MITAOE provide hostel facilities?
A: Yes, the college has separate hostel facilities for boys and girls located near the campus.

Q: What are the hostel fees?
A: The hostel fees vary based on the type of room sharing. For boys, the annual fee is approximately **₹88,000** for three-bed sharing and **₹99,000** for twin-bed sharing. For girls, the fee is around **₹88,000** per year. These fees typically do not include mess charges.

Q: What facilities are available in the hostels?
A: The hostels are equipped with facilities like Wi-Fi, 24x7 security, CCTV surveillance, hot water, filtered drinking water, a study table, and a wardrobe in each room. Common amenities include washing machines, refrigerators, and indoor gaming facilities.

Q: What other facilities are available on campus?
A: The campus is equipped with all necessary academic and recreational facilities, including a large central library, well-equipped laboratories for each department, Wi-Fi connectivity, and canteens.
