# MITAOE Admissions

## Undergraduate (UG) Admissions - B.Tech
MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE) offers a four-year, full-time B.Tech program.

### Eligibility Criteria
- **For Maharashtra State Candidates:** Candidates must have passed the HSC or equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subjects. A minimum of 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) is required in these subjects.
- **For All India Candidates:** Same as above.
- Candidates must have a non-zero score in either **MHT-CET** or **JEE Main (Paper-I)**.

### Admission Process
Admissions are conducted through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) managed by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra. Our DTE Code is **EN6176**.

## Postgraduate (PG) Admissions - M.Tech
MITAOE offers a two-year, full-time M.Tech program.

### Eligibility Criteria
- Candidates must have a Bachelor's Degree in the relevant field of Engineering and Technology from an AICTE approved institution.
- A minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) is required.
- Candidates must have a non-zero positive score in the **GATE** examination.

## Fee Structure
The fee structure is governed by the 'Fees Regulating Authority' of the Government of Maharashtra. The official fee for the Academic Year 2024-25 will be updated upon declaration. For bank loan assistance, the college provides necessary documents after the admission process is completed.
