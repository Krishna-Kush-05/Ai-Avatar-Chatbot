# Department of Computer Engineering

## About the Department
The Department of Computer Engineering at MITAOE was established in 1999. It is one of the most sought-after departments in the institute. We offer a B.Tech in Computer Engineering with an intake of 240 students and an M.Tech in Computer Engineering with an intake of 18 students.

## Vision
To develop competent professionals in the field of Computer Engineering who can meet the challenges of the industry and society.

## Mission
- To provide quality education in Computer Engineering through a well-designed curriculum and state-of-the-art infrastructure.
- To foster a research culture and encourage innovation.
- To collaborate with industries to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

- Computer Networks Lab
