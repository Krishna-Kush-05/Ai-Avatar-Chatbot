# Placements at MITAOE

## Training and Placement Cell
The Training and Placement (T&P) Cell at MITAOE is a dedicated team that works towards providing students with the best career opportunities. The cell organizes various training programs, workshops, and seminars to enhance the employability of students.

## Placement Highlights
- **Highest Package:** Information regarding the latest placement packages is available on the official website and brochures. Typically ranges up to 40-50 LPA.
- **Average Package:** The average package is competitive and reflects the quality of our students. Typically ranges from 6-8 LPA.
- **Consistent Placement Record:** Over 90% of eligible students are placed every year.
