# About MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE)

## Our Vision
To be a premier institution of technical education and research, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship for the benefit of society.

## Our Mission
- To provide a conducive learning environment with a focus on academic excellence and holistic development.
- To promote research, innovation, and industry collaboration.
- To instill ethical values and social responsibility in our students.

## History
Established in 1999, MIT Academy of Engineering is an autonomous institution affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). It is managed by the Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Educational Research (MAEER), Pune. The institution is renowned for its academic discipline, research-oriented learning, and a focus on the overall development of its students.

## Location
MITAOE is located at Dehu Phata, Alandi, Pune, Maharashtra 412105. The campus is spread over a serene and spacious area, providing an excellent environment for learning.
